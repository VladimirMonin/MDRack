## Interpretation

- Scenarios A–D establish application wiring, metadata/filter behavior, timing/frame locators, grouping, degradation and deterministic cleanup.
- Static text vectors are derived only from public fixture text. They are independent of judgments, but they are not a model-quality baseline.
- Lexical and whole-resource similarity cells expose weak correspondence between some frozen query wording and frozen artifact markers. This is retained as an observation rather than hidden by judgment-derived vectors.
- All four hybrid-weight cells are executed over the same 30 frozen hybrid cases. Their metrics are descriptive only:
